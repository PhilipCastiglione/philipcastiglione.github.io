I am a full stack web developer passionate about coding, web apps, software as a service, the internet of things and a bunch of other fun nerd stuff. I mainly write in JavaScript and Ruby but I am interested in heaps of other languages. I also happen to have a BCom (Finance) from a fancy uni and a commercial background in management consulting and higher education, which is pretty neat. But I love programming so that’s what I do. You can't stop me!
This site contains a portfolio of my work. Included are a number of projects that you can look at. If you want to. No pressure. These projects were completed during the (amazing) General Assembly Web Development Immersive course between March to May 2015.
