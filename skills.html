    • Rating Scale

    The ratings on this page use an arbitrary rating scale I have developed to self-assess my technical skills in various areas in order to plan growth and development. The scale is not linear; if you placed a population of people with each skill on this scale, I'd expect relatively high kurtosis and positive skewness.

    As a point of reference, I'd expect a good Senior Developer to have 4 stars in a couple of areas, 3 in several more and have a large number of other skills with some level of proficiency. It is difficult to capture less tangible development, like the ability to pick up new languages or paradigms swiftly based on increased experience, so consider it implied.

    Also, I'm unnecessarily harsh on myself, so if you think I might want you to employ me, maybe just look away now.
