    These self-assessed ratings use an arbitrary rating scale and exist to plan growth and development. The scale is not linear; a population of people with each skill on this scale will have relatively high kurtosis and positive skewness.

    As a point of reference, I'd expect a decent Senior to have 4 stars in a couple of areas, 3 in several more and have a large number of other skills with some level of proficiency. Less tangible capabilities are difficult to capture, like the ability to pick up new languages or paradigms swiftly, so consider them implied by the aggregate.

    This list of skills will come across, to the astute observer, as split personality. This is a reflection of the threaded paths of pragmatism and self-realisation that exist on the journey towards my ultimate, frequently refined goal.

    Finally, I'm unnecessarily harsh on myself, so if you think I might want you to employ me, maybe just look away now.
